About Us

Pick n Pay is a South African retail business that was founded in 1967 by Raymond Ackerman. He purchased four small stores in Cape Town and built them into a major supermarket chain, which later went public in 1968. Today, Pick n Pay operates through various store formats, including Pick n Pay and Boxer, and has a significant online presence. The company also has a presence in several other African countries and owns a stake in Zimbabwean supermarket chain, TM Supermarkets.
